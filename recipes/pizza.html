Pizza

Description

Pizza is a delicious dish of bread, tomato sauce, and whatever toppings your heart desires. It originated in Italy but is also very popular in American cities, such as New York, Chicago, and Detroit

Pizzas are usually round, but sometimes they are square or even heart shaped for special occasions. My favorite pizza is pepperoni and olive.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperonis
  • Black olives

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Gather your pizza ingredients.
  3. Build the pizza.
  4. Bake away.
  5. Cool & serve.
